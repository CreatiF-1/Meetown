What is Bracketly?

Bracketly is a new videocall experience designed to gather people online. Smart future service in which work, study, verbal, physical and social interaction from home, can be similar to real life.

CORE FEATURES I FOCUS ON ARE:

Hangout with Friends

We tried to recreate those in a virtual environment interactions that characterize us as people.

Host a conference

Create any space you want, such as conference rooms, offices, apartments, universities, and parties.

Virtualize your university

Teach from a virtual classroom. Lecture, take questions, and split into discussion groups dynamically with the ease of a real classroom.

All organized

Spaces organized for better work. Interact with the objects in the scene as you would in real life. Each object has a specific function. Imagine being in an extraordinary meeting and not having your documents on hand, I'm sure it's not pleasant. Imagine how much time you would save by having your documents, notes and researches just a click away, in a single online platform.
Easy, isn't

Focus on whats important

Interacting with objects has never been so quick and easy. Create and share documents, brainstorm with our whiteboards or divide your work group dynamically and quickly, all in one platform. Having everything on hand will save your time and be more productive.

ABOUT

Bracketly is a project by Francesco Cantinelli. It was created at QIAD in 2020 as my thesis.
